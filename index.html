IslandLinkBus

IslandLinkBus is a live intercity bus booking and operations platform. I work across its customer booking flow, administration system, data model, payments, integrations, and operational tooling, translating real transportation rules into dependable production software.

  • Modernized core booking and administration workflows with Nuxt, Vue, Laravel, and MariaDB for a platform processing 200–300 daily bookings across 45 active intercity bus routes.
  • Engineered a configurable six-level RBAC and member-management system, including sysop-controlled page, route, menu, and CRUD permissions; hierarchy-aware front-end ghost login; account-state and password controls; and auditable iPoints adjustments.
  • Built a dynamic route, connection, and pricing engine supporting cities and bus stops, intermediate connections, operating schedules, prohibited transfers, multi-hop fares, booking cut-offs, taxes, fees, capacity rules, and configurable volume and early-booking discounts.
  • Delivered resilient payments and rewards workflows with cash, iPoints, and split-payment paths, package and invoice administration, reward reporting, and a Moneris recovery check that protects approved transactions when local processing fails.
  • Automated transportation operations through driver and on-call scheduling, detailed manifests, driver logs, bus and location management, maintenance histories, and scheduled-maintenance alerts.
  • Created operational and financial reporting for role-specific reconciliation, trip summaries, future travel and receivables, tax ledgers, passenger activity, average fares, load factor, and seat- and passenger-kilometre yield.
  • Integrated customer and partner workflows with toggleable Twilio confirmations and reminders, targeted delay broadcasts, administrator-published news releases, and restricted partner accounts for same-day booking and custom route pricing.
